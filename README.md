## 🛠️ How I Think About AI

Coming from Solutions Engineering and Professional Services, I've learned that the hardest part of AI adoption isn't the technology — it's the **last mile**: getting the right people to trust it, use it consistently, and measure whether it's actually working.

The tools I build reflect that. Every project here is designed to be:
- **Explainable** — clear README, documented prompts, no black boxes
- **Practical** — solves a real problem a real team faces
- **Extensible** — built to be forked, adapted, and integrated
